About ClassSync

ClassSync is designed to help students and teachers communicate without barriers and keep everyone focused on learning.

The platform brings transparency to student and faculty progress, with insights available in one place for administrators and mentors.

It minimizes workflow for faculty and administrators with a paperless attendance system that uses secure Bluetooth technology. Attendance sessions are fast, reliable, and recorded in real time.

The application is optimized for mobile and desktop so it runs smoothly wherever you use it.
